Hibberdene

Hibberdene is a small coastal town located on the south coast of KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa. It's about 97 km south of Durban, a popular city in the region. The town is known for its beautiful beaches and serves as a quiet holiday destination, particularly for people looking to enjoy the Indian Ocean coastline.

Fun things to do

Beaches: Hibberdene is famous for its Blue Flag beach, which means it's recognized for safety, cleanliness, and environmental management. This makes it a popular spot for swimming, snorkeling, and fishing.

Tourism: It attracts many visitors during the holiday seasons, and the town offers various accommodations ranging from hotels to holiday homes. The beach also has a popular caravan park.

Nature: Besides the beach, the town is surrounded by lush natural scenery and wildlife. Nearby, you can find forests, rivers, and nature reserves for hiking and bird watching.
